Found
Design, Concept
2021
Email ↗ Read.cv ↗ LinkedIn ↗
Found is a conceptual live music streaming app. Found enables creators to live stream music sessions with voice, music or video to their fanbase allowing them to interact via live comments and joint voice hosting. The app uses Apple Music and Spotify APIs to stream music removing issues of ownership of music rights.
The Found app incorporates two main flows: streaming and playlist building. Creators can stream using pre-built playlists by them and take requests from the users in real time. Users can interact and save/add these tracks to their playlists which can be used later purely for listening, hosting their own session, or syncing with Apple Music or Spotify.
