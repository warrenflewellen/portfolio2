Seen Sounds is a library archive started in 2018 to chronicle and glorify the art of turning sound into sight and to empower the silent creators.
Seen Sounds has been a passion side project since 2010 in different forms and names but since then has accumulated to a growing social media account and an archive website to collect and document record artwork, videos, photography and design.

The website is a custom React/Gatsby/Prismic build using ThreeJS for visual effects and Airtable for CMS integration with Prismic.

Each record artwork features an interactive 3D hover over effect while music videos feature a 3D cinema room that alters viewpoint based on the cursor position.
